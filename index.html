Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our maintenance android application. This application is intended for private organizational use only, with pre-established accounts managed by the organization.

Important Notice: This application is exclusively for organizational use and is not intended for public access. User accounts are pre-created and managed by the organization.

Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our service:

  • Logs: Our application collects logs related to maintenance activities which are stored on our secure servers.
  • Photos: Images captured through the application are stored on our servers for maintenance documentation purposes.
  • Location Data: The app uses location services on your device for functionality purposes. This location data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect any personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers through this application, as all user accounts are managed internally by the organization.

Required Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: Required to capture maintenance-related photos
  • Location: Required for local device functionality

Data Storage and Retention

All collected data (logs and photos) are stored on our secure servers for an indefinite duration. This long-term retention is necessary for maintaining comprehensive maintenance records for the organization.

Data Management and Access

As this is an organizational application:

  • Data management and deletion requests are handled internally through organizational procedures
  • User accounts and access are managed exclusively by the organization
  • Data access is restricted to authorized organizational personnel

Age Restriction

This application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13.

Data Sharing and Analytics

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics services. Your data is exclusively used for organizational maintenance documentation and related services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through appropriate organizational channels.
